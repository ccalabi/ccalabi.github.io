Controls:
To pan, use keys:
-Left arrow key: Move negative X
-Right arrow key: Move positive X
-Up arrow key: Move negative Z
-Down arrow key: Move positive Z
- , /< (comma/less than) key: Move negative Y
- . /> (period/greater than) key: Move positive Y

To rotate, use mouse, click and drag in direction you want to rotate.

Please use a browser that supports "canvas"
